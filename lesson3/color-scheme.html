Color Scheme for Weather Site

The proposed color scheme used for the elements on this site are going to reflect a cool and refreshing look. These colors were picked reflect a peaceful, relaxed visual appeal to weather or nature in general.

Website Background - #5CDB95

Header/Footer - #8EE4AF

Navigation Bar - #5CDB95

Text in Navigation Bar, Footer, Header - #05386B

Text in Body of Site - #EDF5E1

Color proposed without designation - #379683

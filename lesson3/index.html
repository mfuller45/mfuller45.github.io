Weather Site Domain Name

The domain name is "Good Time Weather, www.goodtimeweather.com". Currently this domain name is available from source Google Domains.

Weather Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a central site location for residents of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven to check local weather of those towns. The home page will provide curent weather stories and images for all three towns. There will be a individual page for each town that will provide current weather conditions as well as upcoming weather conditions for that town. There will be other related content and articles pertaining to that town. Additionally, there will be a "Storm Center" page for viewers to get the latest up to date storms in the towns and surrounding areas. Also, provided will be a gallery page that viewers can utilize to see images of the weather in the towns.
